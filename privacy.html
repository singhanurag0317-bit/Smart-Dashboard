Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Welcome to Prime Workspace. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

Prime Workspace operates primarily as a client-side application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to external servers. All task data, notes, and preferences are stored locally on your device using localStorage.

2. Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save:

  • Your tasks and their completion status.
  • Your quick notes.
  • Dashboard preferences (e.g., theme settings, focus timer stats).

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's cache or using the "Reset" options within the application.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party libraries for fonts (Google Fonts) and icons (Phosphor Icons). These services may collect basic usage data (IP address) to serve their resources.

4. Contact Us
